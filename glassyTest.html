🔍 How It Works

  • Backdrop Filter: backdrop-filter: blur(20px) creates the frosted glass effect by blurring background content
  • Semi-transparent Background: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.2) provides subtle white overlay
  • Multiple Box Shadows: Layered inset and outset shadows simulate glass depth and lighting
  • Pseudo-elements: ::before and ::after add realistic light reflections on glass edges
  • Border & Radius: Subtle borders and rounded corners complete the modern glass aesthetic
  • Hover Effects: Transform and shadow changes provide interactive feedback
